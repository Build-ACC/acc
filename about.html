Android Code Club


The ACC is a group created by students for students in order to create a supportive environment for coding. The club is run right in the Gregory University, Uturu campus with school approval and supervision.

The coding club is a place for people that want to learn how to code (absolute beginners) and, for people that want to work on their own projects, learn a new language, or just have fun programming in a friendly environment.

We meet two times a week, meeting times are usually agreed upon at our first meeting each semester. Please email dibrinsofor@gmail.com to join as a member or as a volunteer/mentor. You do not have to attend every meeting learning can be done according to your own pace.

Do I need to bring anything? Just yourself. The lab is fitted with computers and internet access. You can use your own laptop if you prefer.
