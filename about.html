**We shifted from in-person events to virtual events during Covid-19 and have left it that way, we meet bi-weekly on Sundays by 8pm (WAT).**

The ACC is a support group created and led by students for people who want to learn to code, work on projects, build cool systems, or just hang around supportive, inspiring people.

Our current strengths lie within software systems, and building for the web. We've collectively built and maintained projects for Gregory University and are open to impactful ideas that can foster learning of group members.

During our in-famous meetings, you can catch us discussing web concepts, pair programming, discussing career development tips, or talking about current projects. We take this very seriously so if you do plan to join, come prepared to share and learn.

Please reach out to Martin nkemakolam.martin@gmail.com or Nnamdi nnamdi6000@gmail.com if you have any questions or just want to collaborate.
